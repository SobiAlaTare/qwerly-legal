# Qwerly Assistant Privacy Policy Last Updated: June 2026 This Privacy Policy explains what information Qwerly Assistant may collect and store. ## 1. Information Collected Qwerly Assistant may store: * Discord User IDs * Roblox User IDs * Roblox Usernames * Verification status * Ticket information * Warning records * Bot usage data necessary for functionality ## 2. Purpose of Collection Information is stored solely to provide features such as: * Roblox account verification * Ticket management * Moderation systems * Community support services ## 3. Data Sharing Qwerly Assistant does not sell or share user data with third parties except where required by law. ## 4. Data Retention Data may be retained as long as necessary for the operation of the bot and community systems. ## 5. Data Removal Users may request removal of linked Roblox account information through available bot commands or by contacting server staff. ## 6. Security Reasonable efforts are made to protect stored information. However, no system can guarantee absolute security. ## 7. Changes This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Continued use of the bot constitutes acceptance of any updates. ## 8. Contact Questions regarding this Privacy Policy may be directed to the Qwerly staff team.
